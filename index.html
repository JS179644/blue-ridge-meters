How To Use This App
  • 1 Tap any site below to open the meter read form
  • 2 Enter the kWh number shown on the meter display
  • 3 Take a photo — include the site number sign and meter face in one shot
  • 4 Tap Save & Next Site — progress saves automatically every time
  • 5 On the last site the button changes to Submit All Sites
  • 6 Safe to close the app anytime — your progress is always saved
Photo tip: Stand back so both the site number sign and meter face are clearly visible in one shot. Required for verification.
